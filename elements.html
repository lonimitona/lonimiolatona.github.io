Data Visualization in R Studio

In this project, I use R Studio to visualize the number of deaths that occurred in care homes in Wales during the COVID Pandemic (March 2020 - April 2022). The data used to plot the graphs and map was retrieved from the Office for National Statistics website. Four different datasets were combined to get information on Death Registrations and COVID-19-related deaths of Care Home residents in Wales for the years 2020, 2021, and 2022. The needed datasets were sorted in MSExcel and further in R Studio. The ggplot package was utilized in visualizing a line chart, overlapping bar chart and area chart. The map was created using the QGIS software. View the code here.

Line Chart

The Line Chart shows the comparison between the 5-year average and Total Deaths in Care Homes in Wales.

Bar Chart

The Overlapping Bar Chart shows the Total Deaths compared to Deaths involving COVID-19 in Care Homes in Wales.

Area Chart

The Area Chart shows the comparison of Total Deaths and COVID-19 Death percentages between different Age Groups in Care Homes in Wales.

Map

The Map shows COVID-19 Deaths in Care Homes in each of the 22 counties of Wales.
