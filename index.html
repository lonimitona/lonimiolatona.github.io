DATA CLEANING AND TRANSFORMATION IN R

In this project, data was analyzed from an online survey questionnaire to determine the intent of expectant mothers to breastfeed their babies. I also performed some statistical analysis including Logistic Regression, Cox Regression, Survival Analysis and Predictive Analysis using the Decision Tree method.

FLU SHOTS DASHBOARD WITH SQL AND TABLEAU

I use SQL to retrieve the data of patients who received Flu shots from an Healthcare systems database for the year 2022. I then upload the generated table into Tableau where I create a visualizations dashboard. The data used was gotten from Synthea which is an open-source synthetic patient generator that models the medical history of synthetic patients.
